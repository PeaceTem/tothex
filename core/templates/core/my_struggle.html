The Beginning

I have always wanted to create something that will be of good use to everyone since I was young. Fortunately, I stumbled upon an idea in 2018 to create a robot, or simply put, an AI, that will help people through their academical journey which will be deployed via a mobile app. It was just an idea, nothing more, nothing less. But I didn't know where to start. After rambling for a long time, I later decided to learn Wordpress(it helps one to create a website with no code at all). After playing with wordpress for a while, I realised that its power is limited, so I went on to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Then, I switched to python in 2020.


I taught for a while. During that period I realised that most, if not all, of my students used to forget whatever they were being taught in less than 2 weeks. So, I carried out a research to know why people forget things easily(I was just browsing the internet though!). Out of all the results I saw online the one that interests me the most is Input-Output-Output-Output model. This model states that the output(revision, practice, rehearsal, or whatever) should be at least 3X more than the Input. That was why I decided to build this app. So people will be able to practice whatever they've learnt.
